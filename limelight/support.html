Details

Spotlight features the ability to create a playlist, add songs from Spotify, and invite others to a playlist using a QR code or a customizable ID. Have a playlist already in Spotify that you want to use? You can use that too.

This app lets anyone upvote songs in a queue so that most desired songs get played first. Additionally, anyone with Spotify-connected accounts can add songs to a playlist.

Ideal for social events such as parties, and is also great for road trips with the friends and family.

How To

Create a Playlist
  1. To create a playlist, you need to be logged in with Spotify by pressing the user icon in the lower left of the screen
  2. Click the "Host Playlist" button (located in the left-most page)
  3. You may use one of your already-created playlists from Spotify or create on from scratch
  4. Once created, you will be brought to the home screen where you can see all of your playlists.
Invite Others to Collaborate
  • You may invite people by QR code or using your own customized ID.
  • You can see your QR code and can edit your ID by pressing the options icon in the top right corner of your playlist screen. Here you will also be able to edit your playlist name.
  • To invite someone with a QR code, tell your friend to navigate to the left-most screen to use the QR scanner.
  • To invite someone with an ID, tell your friend to enter the ID which is also located at the left-most screen.
